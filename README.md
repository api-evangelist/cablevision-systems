# Cablevision Systems (cablevision-systems)
Cablevision Systems was a U.S. cable television company that provided telecommunications and television services (Optimum TV, Optimum Online, Optimum Voice) in the New York metropolitan area. It was acquired by Altice in 2016 and now operates as part of Altice USA under the Optimum consumer brand.
